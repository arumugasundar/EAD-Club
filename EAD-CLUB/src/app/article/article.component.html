The home team arrives

1 jun 2021

Jorginho and Emerson Palmieri have been selected to represent Italy at this summer's European Championship finals.

What is the Special Here?

Jorginho, who started the Champions League final for Chelsea last weekend as the Blues beat Manchester City 1-0, will be looking to build on his 27 international caps in what will be his first experience of a major tournament. Emerson, who made his Italy debut in September 2018, will also be playing at a finals for the first time following Italy's failure to qualify for the 2018 World Cup. The defender has established himself as a regular at left-back under Mancini.
